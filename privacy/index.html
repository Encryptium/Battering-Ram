Privacy Policy

Welcome to Battering Ram. We prioritize your privacy and data protection. Our policy outlines our practices concerning your information.

2. Data Collection

We respect your privacy by not collecting Personally Identifiable Information (PII) directly from our users.

3. Third-Party Services

Third-party services such as Google Analytics and Sentry are utilized to enhance your user experience. These services may collect non-identifiable data under their respective policies.

4. CPRA Compliance

We comply with the CPRA. If you are a California resident, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. For requests, please contact us directly.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Cookies and local storage may be used by us or third-party developers to enhance your experience. You can manage your preferences through our website.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect the data we collect, process, and store.

7. Policy Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change over time, and we will make the updated policy available on our platform.

8. Contact Us

For any questions regarding our privacy practices, please reach out to us.
